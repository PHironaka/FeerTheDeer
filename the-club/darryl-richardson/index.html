TMC Work

10 Deep Lookbook

With Thanksgiving just a day away, 10.Deep has taken the wraps off the first delivery of its Holiday 2014 collection. Dubbed “Town and Country,” the new drop eschews the internet-centric designs of its autumnal releases in favor of a focus on the non-digital world. For the first delivery, 10.Deep looked to the functional apparel that provided much of the stylistic framework for what would eventually evolve into the streetwear of today. The result is a range that draws inspiration from vintage hunting apparel, motocross pieces, and classic workwear while incorporating more contemporary graphics and details — sort of “streetwear for the everyman.”
